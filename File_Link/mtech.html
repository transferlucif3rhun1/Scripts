M.Tech - Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)

M.Tech program in Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) is a two-year journey for aspiring professionals seeking expertise in AI and ML. The program is offered by Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This program focuses on enhancing technical skills to create and deploy AI and ML solutions for real-world applications. Students learn to design adaptable intelligent systems. Furthermore, the program encourages research in AIML, fostering innovation and offering students opportunities to contribute to the advancement of this rapidly evolving field.
