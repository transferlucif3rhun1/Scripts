Master of Computer Applications

The MCA program at the Manipal University Jaipur is a two-year programme spanning over four semesters, this programme aims to provide interface between computer studies and applications. With vastly experienced, highly motivated and dedicated faculty and staff members along with award winning infrastructure, MCA is determined to quench the thirst of knowledge of inquisitive and enthusiastic youngsters. In order to upkeep the high standard of education, the academic syllabus is designed in league with the latest trends and technologies in the field of Computer Science. The coursework is designed to be flexible and wide-ranging, incorporating the cutting edge and ensuring that the students have a firm grasp on the core fundamentals.
